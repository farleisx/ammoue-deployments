Over 60 Years of Excellence

Since our inception in 1965, Zbeda Group has been at the forefront of Israel’s automotive aftermarket and logistics industry. We've built a legacy of precision, reliability, and innovation, constantly evolving to meet the dynamic needs of our partners and customers. Our commitment to excellence drives us forward, ensuring we deliver world-class solutions across every facet of our operations.

1965: Founding Vision

Zbeda Group established, beginning its journey as a trusted automotive partner.

1990s: Strategic Expansion

Diversification into advanced logistics and new automotive divisions.

2010s: Tech Integration

Pioneering automation and digital solutions in supply chain management.

Today: Future-Ready

Leading Israel's automotive aftermarket with next-gen logistics and unparalleled service.
