Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer vegan and gluten-free options?
Yes! We are proud to offer a variety of delicious vegan pastries and milk alternatives (oat, almond, soy) for all our coffee drinks. We also have a selection of gluten-free treats. Please ask our baristas for today's specific options.
What are your typical opening hours?
We are open Monday to Friday from 7 AM to 7 PM, and Saturday to Sunday from 8 AM to 6 PM. Check our social media for any holiday hour changes!
Do you offer catering services or bulk coffee orders?
Absolutely! Aroma Bliss offers catering for events of all sizes, from corporate meetings to private gatherings. We also provide bulk orders of our freshly roasted beans for your home or office. Please contact us via the form below to discuss your specific needs.
